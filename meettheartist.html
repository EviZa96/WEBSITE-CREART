I'm Evita

I'm Evita Zacharioudaki,an artist based on Crete,an island of Greece. From an early age, I was captivated by the vibrant colours and textures of the natural world around me.At the season of "COVID" I was painting with the idea of selling what was creating worldwide.Step to step I was updating my social media about my business and promoting my work direct to my youtube channel uploading content.Today, I bring that same fascination into my work, creating pieces that blend impressionist and realistic styles.



Art has been my sanctuary, especially after a challenging year in 2019. Painting became a way to process my feelings and find joy again. Each stroke of the brush is a journey of curiosity and experimentation, aiming to evoke a sense of peace and inspiration. My work is a reflection of the experiences and emotions of everyday life, infused with a sense of calm and warmth. I believe that art has the power to transform spaces and uplift emotions, turning your home into a personal sanctuary. By bringing a piece of my art into your home, you’re not just decorating—you’re creating an environment that reflects your personality and nurtures your wellbeing. I invite you to explore my collection and find the piece that speaks to you. Let’s create something beautiful together, and bring a touch of colorful calm into your life.
